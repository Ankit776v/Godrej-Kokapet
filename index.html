About Us

GODREJ KOKAPET

Kokapet, Hyderabad

Godrej Kokapet is a stunning contemporary apartment complex located in Kokapet, West Hyderabad.

This newly launched in Hyderabad has tall towers offering spacious 3 and 4 BHK apartments in various configurations.

Spread over a large area, the project has 80% of the open spaces landscaped with lush greenery, providing a peaceful living environment.

Set to launch in 2024, customers can expect to be in their homes by December 2028.

Godrej Kokapet is the perfect blend of modern living and natural beauty, and offers residents a unique lifestyle at one of the most sought after places in Hyderabad
